## Sharing Assistants

On the assistant detail page, click the Share tab > Manage Shared Users. Select users from your organization.

Shared users can:
- View the assistant's configuration
- Chat with the assistant
- See test results

Shared users **cannot**: edit, delete, or publish the assistant.

**Requirement:** Sharing must be enabled at the organization level by your admin. If you don't see the Share tab, contact your admin.

## Sharing Knowledge Bases

Click the Share button on the KB list. Shared KBs are read-only — other educators can connect them to their assistants but cannot modify content.

## Sharing Prompt Templates

On the Prompt Templates tab, click Share on a template. Toggle "Shared Templates" to see templates others have shared.

## Shared with Me

The "Shared with Me" tab on Learning Assistants shows all assistants shared with you by other educators. You can view and chat but not edit or publish.
