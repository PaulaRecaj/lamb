## Login

Open your LAMB instance URL in a browser. Enter your email and password, click Login.

If your institution uses LTI Creator integration, you can access LAMB directly from your LMS without a separate login.

## Signup

If self-registration is enabled, click Sign up on the login page. You need:
- Email, name, password
- The signup key provided by your organization administrator

## Dashboard

After login you see the Dashboard with:
- Your name, organization, and role
- My Resources: counts of assistants (total + published), knowledge bases, rubrics, prompt templates
- Shared with Me: resources other educators shared with you

## Navigation

The top bar has:
- **Learning Assistants** — create, edit, test, publish assistants
- **Sources of Knowledge** (dropdown) — Knowledge Bases and Rubrics
- **Admin** — organization and user management (admin only)
- **Language selector** (EN/ES/CA/EU) — switch interface language

## User Roles

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| Member | Create assistants, KBs, rubrics. Publish own assistants. |
| Org Admin | All member permissions + manage org settings and members |
| System Admin | All permissions across all organizations |

## Organizations

Every user belongs to one organization. Organizations isolate users, assistants, KBs, and LLM provider configuration. The organization admin configures which AI models are available.
